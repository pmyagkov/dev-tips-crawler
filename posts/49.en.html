DevTools: Cycle through the DevTools panels with a keyboard shortcut ===== https://habrastorage.org/files/3aa/50a/19a/3aa50a19aaef41759852235c0e35b29b.gif =====

You can use Cmd + ] (or Cmd + [) to cycle through the panels in the DevTools. Also as a reminder, you can open DevTools with Cmd + Alt + i.
